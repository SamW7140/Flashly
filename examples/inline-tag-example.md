## What is a cell?
The basic structural and functional unit of all living organisms.

## What is the function of mitochondria?
Mitochondria are the powerhouse of the cell, responsible for 
producing ATP through cellular respiration.

## What is an alkane?
A saturated hydrocarbon with single bonds only.

## What is an alkene?
An unsaturated hydrocarbon containing at least one carbon-carbon double bond.
